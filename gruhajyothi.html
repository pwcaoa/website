Gruha Jyothi Registraion Process [Free Electricity Under 200 Units]


Last date for Registration is Due to earlier technical issues Govt is still accepting Gruha Jyothi Scheme Registration. Hurry up to avail, till it lasts.

1. Go to Gruha Jyothi Scheme Registration Page https://sevasindhugs.karnataka.gov.in/gsdn/. Click on check box, enter captcha code and click on Agree




2. Enter your Aadhaar Number.




3. In EKYC Service page, again enter your Aadhaar Number and click on Get OTP.




4. Enter Aadhaar OTP received on your mobile and Click on Submit.




5. Verify your name, Select BESCOM (ESCOM section), Enter Account ID (to be found on BESCOM bill), Name and address will auto populate, Enter Occupancy Type and Mobile Number. Click on Get OTP. Enter OTP received on your mobile and Submit.




6. In final page you will receive confirmation. Print or Download acknowledgement for your reference.
