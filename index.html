About PWC

Provident Welworth City is the sprawling 41 acre (+2.8 acre CA land) gated community, with more than 70% open space located in the north of Bangalore, in close proximity to the Kempagowda International Airport, on the Yelahanka-Doddaballapur Main Road. This fast-growing region of Bangalore has seen tremendous infrastructural development over the last few years.

With 3360 beautiful homes, Provident Welworth City is one of the largest residential condominium in Bangalore.

One of the best property in North Bangalore for Investments, even more, own this property to call it your HOME, with resort like facilities.

Provident Welworth City Apartment Owners Association (PWCAOA) is the association that manages Provident Welworth City condominium.

 
  • PWCAOA administrative office is located in club house building, within PWC condominium.
  • Elected Office Bearers / Board of Managers administer the association as per Karnataka Apartment Owners Act [KAOA] 1972.
